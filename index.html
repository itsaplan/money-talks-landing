The problem

The hardest part of planning something together isn't the logistics. It's the budget.

Some people stretch their budget and don't say so. Others hold back from suggesting somewhere cheaper. The budget conversation never really happens — and the trip, the dinner, or the weekend ends up being tense for reasons nobody names.

🤐

The silence

Nobody wants to be the one who says "that's too much for me." So the group defaults to whoever spoke first — usually the highest spender.

💸

The overspend

Plans get locked in before budgets get aligned. Someone ends up putting it on a credit card and quietly resenting the whole thing for months.

💔

The group chat goes quiet

Someone pulls out last minute. Another stops replying in the group chat. Years of closeness quietly bruised over a €50 gap nobody felt safe naming out loud.
